## Description

**Stock Portfolio Assistant** is a cross-platform portfolio tracker that aggregates holdings from multiple brokerages into a single, unified dashboard.

If you invest across different platforms — A-shares in one broker, US stocks in Tiger, HK stocks in Futu, funds in Alipay — you know the pain of switching between apps to figure out your total P&L. This tool solves that.

### Key Features

- **Multi-broker aggregation** — Futu, Tiger, Xueqiu, A-share brokers, Alipay funds, futures accounts
- **Real-time quotes** — A-shares, HK stocks, US stocks, funds, commodity futures
- **Auto currency conversion** — USD, HKD, CNY automatically converted for total P&L
- **Short position support** — Correct P&L calculation for futures shorts
- **Visual analytics** — Pie charts (by stock/platform), daily P&L, historical trend line
- **Transaction tracking** — Buy more / sell with realized P&L recording
- **Privacy first** — All data stored locally on your device (SQLite), zero tracking, no ads
- **Cross-platform** — Web, Android, macOS from a single Flutter codebase

### Tech Stack

Flutter, Drift (SQLite), Riverpod, Supabase Auth, Vercel

## Maker Comment

Hi Product Hunt!

I built Stock Portfolio Assistant to scratch my own itch. As someone investing across 5 different broker apps in China, Hong Kong, and US markets, I was tired of opening multiple apps and doing mental math with currency conversions just to know my total returns.

So I built this — a simple tool that puts everything in one screen. No cloud sync of your financial data, no analytics tracking, no ads. Just your portfolio, clearly laid out.

It's a solo project — I designed, coded, and deployed everything myself using Flutter. The web version is live at stockportfolio.company if you want to try it out.

I'd love to hear your feedback, especially if you also invest across multiple markets. What features would make this more useful for you?
